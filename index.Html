Proclamation: Irvenian Grand Exploration

The clockwork heart of Irvenia beats faster. Steam rises, gears grind, and the veil of mystery parts. The Adventurers’ Guild seeks valiant agents to uncover the forgotten wonders and mechanized horrors across the land. Treasures lie buried, ruins hum with arcane current, and danger ticks ever louder.

Known Factions of Influence

The Adventurers Guild – Honorable brokers of coin and chaos. Contracts, bounties, and legacy await.

The Vanguard – Gilded knights, custodians of balance. The weight of duty fuels their steamforged war machines.

Sons of the Plague – Alchemists of affliction. In rot, they find renewal.

Alchemia – Scribes of science, inventors divine. Seekers of forbidden transmutations and eternal life.

The Blood Monarch Cult – Crimson worshippers. Bound by ancient oaths and vampiric thirsts.

Academia Elementia – Elemental academics wielding primal forces and technomagical fusion.

COG Rebellion – Sentient constructs. Once servants, now sovereigns.

The Regions of Irvenia

The Capital – Humming with gearwork and golden steam. The Guild’s spires overlook merchant airships and soot-choked alleys.

Umbravale – A fog-shrouded realm of whispers, shadows, and forbidden machines.

Euchridia – Elegant, fractured, forested. Its elven tinkers balance arcane lore and steambound curiosity.

Lantis – Beneath the sea, coral turbines churn currents and secrets alike.

Fjordvale – Steel meets snow. Ice-fortresses house hardy clans and steam-borne sledcrafts.

Chaotica – The impossible made real. A land where steam runs backward and gravity bends.

Commission #001: Weeping Marsh Anomaly

Issued by Guild Master Kian Ravienhart

The party is summoned by Guild Master Kian Ravienhart, a renowned figure in New Hestia, to embark on a Grand Exploration across the uncharted territories of Irvenia. Their first commission: investigate the eerie and treacherous region known as the Weeping Marsh—a vast, festering swampland teeming with dangerous creatures. Rumors speak of Swamp Beasts, Werewolves, elusive Fog-Crawlers, and a chilling cryptid known only as the Mudman. The adventurers are tasked with traveling to a small, remote outpost on the marsh's edge: Settlement Dania. Once a quiet frontier village, Dania has recently fallen silent. The guild suspects something unnatural is stirring in the swamp—and it’s up to the party to uncover the truth.
